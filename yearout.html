The year out pages

Susan and I resigned in 2001, took Nick out of school and travelled the system for a year on B-Hive.

During that time I ran a website - would nowadays be called a blog - using a 28k (erratic!) mobile phone link, with logs, pictures and current boat location

Unfortunately this website (there were actually 2 on different ISPs) was lost when I changed ISPs, and subsequently through various hard disk crashes I lost the backups also.

I have manage to recreate most of it using web.archive.org, but any links which are struck through are currently not operational

Hope you enjoy it here!
