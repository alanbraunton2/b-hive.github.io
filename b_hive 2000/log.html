Saturday 12/8/00
10:30-?, 14:00-17:30
 		   Rear tyre punctured - so walked in to buy pump, spare inner tube and other shopping. Back up 3 of Audlem locks, for lunch/shop/pub/water. Up the rest of the locks by 3:45, moored above Adderley. Fridge has stopped - no flame. Cleaned - now lights but flame very small
