B-Hive is a 62 foot narrowboat now moored in our home town of  Devizes.

Built by Jonathon Wilson, she was launched at Jericho, Oxford on Valentines day 1989 as a sailaway
then subsequently fitted out by ourselves.

Since then she has travelled over most of the waterway system, notably excluding the Leeds and Liverpool canal,
because the builder was generous and gave us an extra 7 inches - which means B-Hive is just too long to fit diagonally in the broad 60 foot locks - annoying!

These pages give an idea of the voyages - usually 6 weeks every summer, with additional school holiday trips as well, organised by year.

Later I hope to add the voyages of our previous boat, Celandine B - a 45 foot built by Rugby boat builders..
