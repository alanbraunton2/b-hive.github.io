B-Hive technical data

Length 62 feet 7 inches
Beam 6 feet 10 inches
Draught 1 foot 10 static
Built 1989 by Jonathon Wilson
Steelwork 10/6/4
Engine Thorneycroft 108 - marinised  BMC 1.8, keel cooled
Gearbox Newage PRM160 VR2 driving an 18x14 propeller
Exhaust Wet exhaust, fed by Jabsco pump driven from crankshaft
Batteries 4 auxiliary, 1 engine starter
Water tank capacity About 240 gallons
Fuel tank capacity About 150 litres
